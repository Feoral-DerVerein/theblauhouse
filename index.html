About The Blau House

Meaningful human connection experiences through curated events.

We believe that real connection doesn't happen in crowded conference rooms or over business cards. It happens at a table, under the sunset, with the right people. The Blau house is conversation & connection with intention.

The Journey is the launch event, a private dinner in Ibiza for women leaders, founders, investors, and creatives from across Europe. Just the right room at the right time with the right people.
